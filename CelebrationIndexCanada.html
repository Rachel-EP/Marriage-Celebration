Rachel and Steven Marriage Celebration

Get Ready for July 3rd 2025!

Welcome to the marriage celebration of Rachel and Steven Echevarria! We are so excited to celebrate our special day with you in Cambridge at the gorgeous Cambridge Mill. As most of our guests will be travelling from out of town we've put together all the information you'll need to make your trip and our day as seamless and enjoyable as possible. From accommodation options we recommend to suggestions for local dining and activities, we've got you covered.

At the top of each page you'll have a navigation bar:

  • Itinerary: Goes over plans for every day including the day of the celebration. This will be updated as we get closer to the day and continue to make plans with the venue.
  • Places to Stay: This is where you will find information on hotels in the area, as well as nearby restaurant options.
  • The Celebration: On this page is currently information on the venue but eventually this page will be updated to include inforamtion on the food and dresss code etc.
  • Gift Registry: Currently this page is almost empty but will be updated once our registry is built and complete.
  • RSVP: Currently this page is empty but once the RSVP is available it will be on this page but for now treat this as a save the date.

Just a note that this website is still very much a work in progress and will continue to be updated as we continue to plan and talk with our venue. Please note as well that all prices listed on the website and that are linked to on other sites are subject to change over the coming months.

Please let us know if you have any questions or concerns!
