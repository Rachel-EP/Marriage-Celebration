Places to Stay

Since most of our guests are coming from all over Ontario most will require places to stay. The hotels listed below are nearby our venue (The Cambridge Mill) and includes options for all budgets.

Best Western Plus Cambridge

730 Old Hespeler Rd. Cambridge, ON.

The Best Western Plus Cambridge Hotel offers a comfortable and convenient stay. This hotel combines modern amenities with reliable service, making it a popular choice for those visiting the region. A stay here includes amenities such as complimentary full breakfast, free parking, an indoor pool and fitness center.

The Best Western is approximately a 15 minute drive away from the venue. Please note the Best Western is NOT pet friendly.

The Laundry Rooms Cambridge

15 Glebe St. Cambridge, ON.

The Laundry Rooms Cambridge is a boutique hotel that offers a unique blend of modern amenities and historical charm. Located in the heart of Cambridge, Ontario, this property is designed to feel like a home away from home. The hotel is housed in a beautifully restored heritage building, maintaining its historical character while providing contemporary comforts.

Each suite features fully equipped kitchens, in-suite laundry, and high-speed Wi-Fi. Guests can also enjoy amenities such as a fitness center, co-working spaces, and a rooftop terrace with stunning views of the city. However, on-site parking can be limited, so guests may need to use nearby public parking facilities.

The Laundry Rooms is approximately a 3 minute drive or a 15 minute walk to the venue. Please note that this hotel IS pet friendly.
